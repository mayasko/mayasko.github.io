About

I am a researcher with the Power Electronics Team of the ELECTA research Group at KU Leuven and EnergyVille research center (Belgium). I was a visiting researcher at the Power Electronics, Machines and Control (PEMC) Research Institute at the University of Nottingham (UK). I conducted my PhD research on charging efficiency for electric vehicles with a focus on advancing the practical effficiency from converter, architecture and vehicle perpectives. I am also actively involved in Education (teaching and master thesis supervision), industrial projects (EU Horizon, Interreg, VLAIO, ETF, etc...) and professional communities.

My main research interests are in electrical energy systems with particular emphasis on energy efficiency and modular power electronics.

if you are interested in (academic/industry) collaboration, please feel free to contact me.
